Parents in the family

The role of parents in the family

Parents play a crucial role in shaping their children's character. They have to provide good care and upbringing, give enough attention, and teach good values and morals. Additionally, parents must provide emotional and social support to their children. This support is vital to help children grow and develop optimally. Parents are also responsible for creating a safe and comfortable environment for the family. They must ensure that the family has a decent place to live, enough nutritious food, and basic needs are met. Moreover, parents must help their children learn and develop in various aspects such as education, social skills, and life skills.


In addition to providing care and support, parents must also play an active role in fulfilling their family responsibilities. They must work together with their partners in making important decisions, such as family spending and financial planning. Moreover, parents must instill values of hard work, independence, and responsibility in their children. However, the role of parents in the family must be balanced with an equally important role from the husband-wife partnership. Both must communicate and work together in carrying out family responsibilities. This way, the family can create a harmonious and healthy environment for all family members.
